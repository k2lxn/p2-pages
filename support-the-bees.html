About Support the Bees

With the Support the Bees Campaign, Pollinator Partnership will expand its programs, and guarantee its sustainability for the benefit of pollinators.

The campaign will raise $5 Million over three years, though funds may be received over as much as the next five years to accomodate donors. This funding will be raised primarily as multi-year gifts, beginning in the Spring of 2016.

20 Years of Pollinator Partnership

  • For over 16 years, Pollinator Partnership has given vision to pollinator efforts around the globe through its North American Pollinator Protection Campaign (NAPPC).
  • P2’s Ecoregional Planting Guides provide recipes for success across the continent.
  • P2’s Pollinator Week, now in its eighth year, was created by P2 in order to raise awareness and action in celebration of pollinators.
  • The annual Pollinator Week Poster illustrates P2 ability to make conserva on resonate with new audiences.

P2: A History of Success

Joining and supporting this effort are some of the largest businesses and most powerful voices in the country, including First Lady Michelle Obama, who announced the Million Pollinator Garden Challenge at the White House earlier this year.

Pollinator Partnership worked intimately with White House staff on President Obama's Presidential Memorandum on Pollinators, which in turn heralded the 2015 release of the first-ever Federal Strategy for the Health of Honey Bees and other Pollinators.
