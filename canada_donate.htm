WHERE WE WORK - CANADA

Your donations help us help pollinators! Pollinator Partnership Canada is a registered Canadian notforprofit organization that works to enhance pollinator habitat, encourages research and policy actions that help protect pollinators, and provides outreach and education materials throughout Canada. Donate directly to our Canadian pollinator programs through our parent organization Pollinator Partnership. Thank you!

Canadian donors wishing to receive a tax receipt fillable with Canada Revenue Agency should make a donation to our pollinator programs via Seeds of Diversity Canada – our local registered charity collaborator. Click on the link below, select “other payments” and list Pollinator Partnership Canada in the “notes” section.
