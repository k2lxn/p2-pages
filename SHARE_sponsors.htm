S.H.A.R.E. Sponsorship Opportunities

We at the Pollinator Partnership are a-buzz with excitement about the on-the-ground effect of S.H.A.R.E.!  Please consider joining the effort and sponsoring S.H.A.R.E.  Together we collective make real on-the-ground changes that can positively effect conservation and protection of pollinating animals, which are so important to produce the goods on which we depend.  As a S.H.A.R.E. sponsor, you help bring the importance of pollinator health to the forefront of a vast diversity of audiences, including educators, government agencies, for-profit and non-profits, gardeners, beekeepers, industry, and more! Your organization and the Pollinator Partnership will be a force that will not only catapult pollinator health to a new public awareness, but will also bring visibility to your brand to an attentive and responsive audience. For more information about sponsorship, please email SHARE@pollinator.org.

Give to the S.H.A.R.E. Program

Your donation is a great investment and will go directly to ensure the SHARE program keeps going. Thank you for your support and making our work possible.
